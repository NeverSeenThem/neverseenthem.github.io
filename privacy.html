Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

NeverSeenThem does not collect any personal information from TikTok users or viewers. The app operates solely on behalf of the account owner and processes only video files and post metadata.

2. How Information Is Used

Video content and metadata are used exclusively to publish posts to the owner's TikTok account via the TikTok Content Posting API. No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses the TikTok API in accordance with TikTok's Terms of Service and Developer Policies. No other third-party data sharing occurs.

4. Data Retention

No user data is retained. Video files are stored locally by the account owner and are not transmitted to any third party other than TikTok via their official API.
